Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 25, 2025

Wizard Parking LLC (“Wizard Parking,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) provides a technology platform to help users find and reserve parking spots. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our website, mobile applications, and services (collectively, the "Platform").

By using our Platform, you agree to the collection, use, and sharing of your information as described in this policy. This Privacy Policy is incorporated into our Terms of Service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, information we collect automatically when you use our Platform, and information from other sources.

a. Information You Provide to Us:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and password.
  • Vehicle Information: We collect information about your vehicle, such as its make, model, year, and license plate number, to facilitate your reservations.
  • Payment Information: When you add a payment method, we and our third-party payment processor collect information such as your credit card number, expiration date, and security code. For security, we only store a tokenized version and the last four digits of your card.
  • Communications: When you contact our customer support, we collect the information you provide in your communications.

b. Information We Collect Automatically:

  • Usage Information: We collect information about your interactions with our Platform, such as the parking spots you search for, the reservations you book, and the pages you view.
  • Device and Log Information: We collect information from your device, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, and mobile network information.
  • Location Information: With your permission, we may collect your device's precise geolocation to help you find nearby parking spots. You can disable this in your device settings, but it may limit the functionality of our Platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various business purposes, including:

  • To Provide and Improve Our Platform: To operate our services, process your reservations and payments, and personalize your experience.
  • For Communication: To send you transaction-related communications (like booking confirmations and reminders) and respond to your support requests.
  • For Marketing: With your consent, to send you promotional emails and offers. You can opt out at any time.
  • For Security and Fraud Prevention: To protect the integrity of our Platform and protect against fraudulent transactions.
  • To Fulfill Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Parking Operators: We share necessary information (such as your name, vehicle information, and reservation time) with the parking facility operators to validate and facilitate your reservation.
  • With Service Providers: We share information with third-party vendors and service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing, data analytics, and hosting. These providers are obligated to protect your information and can only use it for the services they are providing to us.
  • For Business Transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information to law enforcement or other third parties if we believe it is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect our rights or property, or prevent harm.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with other third parties when we have your explicit consent to do so.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights and choices regarding your information:

  • Account Information: You can review and update your account information at any time by logging into your account.
  • Email Communications: You can unsubscribe from our promotional emails by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in the email. You will still receive essential transactional emails related to your account and reservations.
  • Location Information: You can control location tracking by adjusting the settings on your mobile device.
  • Data Access and Deletion: Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access to or deletion of your personal information. To make such a request, please contact us at the email below.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to help operate our Platform, analyze usage, and for advertising purposes. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures, including administrative, technical, and physical safeguards, to protect your information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access. However, no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

7. Use by Children

Our Platform is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

8. International Users

Wizard Parking is based in the United States. If you are using our Platform from outside the U.S., please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the U.S. By using our Platform, you consent to this transfer.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by posting the updated policy on our Platform and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

10. Contact Information
